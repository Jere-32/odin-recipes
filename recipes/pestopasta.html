Pestopasta

Description

Pesto pasta is easy to make and full of flavor. It tastes good hot or cold!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Paprika
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Your chosen type of pasta
  • pesto
  • spices

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into pieces
  2. Cut paprika and cherry tomatoes
  3. Add spices
  4. Cook chicken in a skillet
  5. While cooking chicken boil your pasta
  6. Add paprika and tomatoes to skillet and let them cook for a while
  7. Add pesto to skillet
  8. drain pasta
  9. Mix everything together and serve
