Makaronilaatikko

Description

Signtature Finnish dish!

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • eggs
  • milk
  • spices
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook your pasta al dente
  2. While cooking pasta, brown the ground beef and season
  3. Add onion with ground beef
  4. Mix eggs and milk together
  5. Mix pasta and ground beef together
  6. Pour eggmilk to your mix
  7. Cover with cheese
  8. Cook in preheated oven for 40 minutes
  9. Enjoy!
